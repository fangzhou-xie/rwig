# NYT headlines by economic policy uncertainty

NYT headlines by economic policy uncertainty

## Usage

``` r
headlines
```

## Format

### headlines

A data frame with 18,507 rows and 2 columns:

- headline:

  Headline of the NYT News

- ref_date:

  Date of the News

## Source

www.nytimes.com
